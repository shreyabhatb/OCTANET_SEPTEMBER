Our Almond Selection

Organic Nonpareil Almonds

Organic Nonpareil almonds are renowned for their mild, sweet, and buttery flavor. They have a smooth and slightly crunchy texture, which makes them a versatile choice for various uses. These almonds are commonly used in snacking, baking, and garnishing due to their delicate flavor and attractive appearance. They are often favored for their consistent quality and easy adaptability to different recipes.

Organic Carmel Almonds

Organic Carmel almonds offer a slightly sweet and mildly nutty flavor, paired with a firm and crunchy texture. This makes them an excellent choice for roasting and baking, as their robust texture holds up well in various culinary applications. They are also used in confections and snacks, providing a satisfying crunch and a subtle sweetness that enhances both savory and sweet dishes.

Organic Butte Almonds

Organic Butte almonds are characterized by their rich, sweet taste with a hint of bitterness. Their crunchy and firm texture makes them ideal for snacking and for use in almond butter. The slightly bitter undertone balances their sweetness, offering a complex flavor profile that complements both sweet and savory recipes.
